## 🚀 About VTS SPACE

> *"It's for everyone."* — We build technology that is **accessible, affordable, and impactful** for every student, institution, and business in India.

**VTS SPACE** (Vansh Technology Services) is an MSME & GeM-registered IT agency based in **Sikandra, Uttar Pradesh**, dedicated to digitizing education and delivering enterprise-grade software solutions for the modern India.

- 🎓 Digitizing polytechnic education across Uttar Pradesh (BTEUP ecosystem)
- 📱 Crafting cross-platform mobile & web solutions with Flutter & Firebase
- 🤖 Integrating AI/LLM capabilities into real-world products
- 🏫 Partnered with **Government Polytechnic Sikandra** for EdTech innovation
